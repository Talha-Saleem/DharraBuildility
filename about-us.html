Who we are

Dharra Buildility started in 2008 with a commercial project in Faisalabad and since then, there is no looking back. With an idea to make the best value our of money and side by side, giving back to society, the company expanded its spectrum by deepening and widening its presence. We are now present in Lahore, Faisalabad, Gujranwala with ISLAMABAD as its hub. Our work experience describes our wide spectrum in terms of function, size, location, building systems, materials technology, and clients. We have worked with leading groups of Pakistan in different sectors providing different services.

  • Our Mission

    To be the leading services providers in building and construction that ensures higher ROI, value for money and ensuring complete customer satisfaction through novelty and with unprecedented quality of work.

  • Our Vision

    To be the most trusted and reliable builders and developers nationally with that ensure best value for money not only for the Investors but also for the End Users.

  • Our Values

    At Dhara buildility, we strongly believe to develop and own values higher than anything else. We strongly believe in Client services at heart, Transparency, Integrity, and Authenticity/Novelty
